Intro

Hiring a team of guards can be a huge headache. Outsourcing is much safer and cheaper. Why?

It’s Reliable

We reduce the human factor to the minimum. The identity of people responsible for your security is confidential, and all personal contact with anyone but you or your contact person is ruled out.

That’s how we eliminate the chance of designful crime based on personal relations and the human factor.

It’s Affordable

Salaries grow fast. And you also need to spend your resources to manage the team. When you leave the job to us, we do the same remotely, with an optimized process saving a lot of resources. Outsourcing costs far less and offers you to cover a bigger area.

We stay in touch with you or your contact person via phone, chat or email, 24/7, 365 days a year. If a crime in your facility can be deterred, we’ll take care of it.

You can relax and just look through our hourly and daily reports.
